ctrl - ] vim-go: go to definition

ctrl - t vim:go back from definition

ctrl - o vim:jumps: jump to previous in jump list

ctrl - i vim:jumps: jump to last in jump list

:ju[mp] vim:jumps: show jump list

:GoReferrers vim:jumps: show referrences of current cursor definition
